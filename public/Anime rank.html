The best animes according to NikoNi7

My top 3 animes of this and last year

Code Geass (all seasons)

This is an anime that tells the tale of an unfortunate son of a British emperor who fights against his whole family to liberate Japanese territories that they unrightfully occupied. It is a story about a friendship, love and ultimate self-sacrifice for the loved ones.

Attack on Titan

Action and horror anime full of action scenes and emotional bonds between characters. It has many plot twists that helped me stay engaged with the story for many seasons.

Sayonara Zetsubou-sensei

Satirical anime that critiques modern day life and makes fun of social and cultural norms, concepts and its own characters
